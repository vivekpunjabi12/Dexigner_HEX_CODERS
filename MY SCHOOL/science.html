SCIENCE STREAM

The science stream is further divided into 2 branches, namely medical (PCB) and non-medical (PCM) science stream. The difference between the two lies in the choice of the third subject. While Physics and Chemistry are compulsory for both, the medical stream offers Biology as the 3rd compulsory subject, and the non-medical stream offers Mathematics. English is common to all streams and students can take up any optional subject to meet a total of 5 subjects.
